About Our Government

Our server operates under a structured government system designed to ensure fair, transparent, and effective community management. We believe in democratic principles while maintaining the stability and security necessary for a thriving online community.

The server government consists of elected representatives, appointed administrators, and community volunteers who work together to create policies, resolve disputes, and guide the direction of our community. Every decision is made with the best interests of our members in mind.

Governance Policies

Decision Making Process

  • Major policy changes require community input and council approval
  • Administrative decisions are made transparently with public announcements
  • Emergency decisions can be made by administrators with later review
  • All members can propose changes through proper channels

Elections & Appointments

  • Community Council members are elected every 6 months
  • Moderators are appointed based on merit and community trust
  • All positions have term limits to ensure fresh perspectives
  • Voting is open to all members in good standing

Accountability Measures

  • Regular performance reviews for all staff positions
  • Public logs of all administrative actions
  • Appeal process for all disciplinary actions
  • Community feedback mechanisms for staff evaluation

Rights & Protections

  • All members have the right to fair treatment and due process
  • Privacy protection and data security measures
  • Freedom of expression within community guidelines
  • Protection against harassment and discrimination
